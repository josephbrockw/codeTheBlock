Vision

Code the Block envisions a future where Kansas City's underserved students become key stakeholders in the local tech landscape.

Mission

Code the Block accelerates the growth of Kansas City's tech and startup community through engaging and rigorous educational programs.


How We Do This

Code the Block seeks to help bridge the STEM/CS achievement gap by providing quality computer science educational programming to the local Kansas City community. It is our belief that every student should have the opportunity to receive a quality computer science education. Providing meaningful exposure enables students to see two key ideas:

  • Computer science is now universially applicable to nearly every discipline
  • How computer science is one of the most powerful tools through which to see how technology can solve problems in the community.

Our Solution

Code the Block's educational programs aim to create a clear path to success that minimizes the chances that a student becomes overwhelmed, frustrated and quits. We bring together the brightest minds from the education and tech circles to provide students with a quality learning experience in a supportive environment. This will allow us to take students all the way to employment and insure time spent working produces the best results. We seek to provide an empowering educational experience, increasing student engagement and success.

We will leverage this success to form future employment partnerships with tech companies, working towards filling the tech demand gap. In addition to this, we will leverage our success to form partnerships with schools, districts, and community organizations to replicate similar educational programs across the city, multiplying the impact and reach to every student in every community.

As more people gain meaningful tech education and training, we can generate the talent needed to fill the demand gap of tech jobs in Kansas City, stimulating the local economy.

Our Program

Our after school program is designed to introduce students to concepts and languages that will set them up for success as they continue their journey into computer science. Our curriculum is focused on creating early successes for our students where they can start to see the impact that their new skills can have in the real world and on projects they care about. Code the Block’s unique teaching focuses on helping our students develop all skills necessary to be impactful immediately. We select students for our program based on their motivation to grow and develop as a young individual.
